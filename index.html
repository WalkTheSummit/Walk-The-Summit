Who Are We?

We're a student-led organization dedicated to transforming the lives of children in the remote villages of the Himalayas. We are driven by a deep passion to ignite a spark of curiosity and opportunity in these young minds, transcending the limitations of their local schools and connecting them with the vast world beyond, to show them that their dreams can soar as high as the peaks that surround them.


What is our mission?

In the rugged terrain of the Himalayas, where a simple pencil is a precious commodity, where children trek miles just to reach school, we stand as a beacon of hope and progress. That's why we're not just setting up computer labs and bringing Wi-Fi to these villages, we're also empowering these kids with essential 21st century life skills, preparing them for a future where they can thrive and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our efforts extend to supporting local schools initiated by the community itself, often lacking experienced teachers and resources. By providing them with computers and Wi-Fi, we connect them with the outside world, ensuring that every child has access to tools for success regardless of their geographical constraints. We're not just changing lives, we're shaping futures and building bridges between remote communities and the global stage.

Current Projects

Currently, we are supporting a school in Ram Gaon, located 12 kilometers away from Mussoorie. We have already set up Wi-Fi at the school and are now seeking funds of about 80,000-90,000 to provide two to three 40-inch LED screens and laptops for the students. These technological resources will greatly enhance their learning experience.In addition to the hardware, we also plan to conduct virtual summer programs in collaboration with other NGOs. These programs will focus on teaching the students valuable 21st-century skills through the monitors and laptops we provide, all made possible through fundraising efforts.
